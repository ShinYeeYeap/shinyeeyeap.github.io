Jennie Kim

Jennie Kim is a South Korean singer and rapper who is famous as a member of the K-pop group BLACKPINK. She was born in 1996 and spent part of her childhood studying in New Zealand, which helped her become comfortable with English. Jennie is known for her confident stage presence, stylish fashion sense, and strong performance skills.

Many people like her because she can sing and rap in different music styles, especially pop and hip-hop. She debuted as a solo artist in 2018 with the song “SOLO,” which became very popular and showed her independent and powerful image. Besides music, Jennie is also well known in the fashion industry and has worked with international luxury brands. Overall, she is considered one of the most influential K-pop artists today.
